DESCRIPTION (4000 characters max):
Take control of your gig work finances with Bookd - the essential companion app built specifically for gig workers, freelancers, and independent contractors.

EFFORTLESS INCOME TRACKING
Log every gig with detailed client and event information. Track what you're owed versus what you've been paid. See your earnings at a glance with our intuitive calendar view and comprehensive dashboard.

SMART EXPENSE MANAGEMENT
Record business expenses instantly with our mobile-optimized forms. Link expenses to specific gigs for better organization. Track everything from parking fees to supplies, all categorized for easy tax preparation.

AUTOMATIC MILEAGE CALCULATION
Let Bookd do the math for you. Enter your starting and ending addresses, and we'll calculate your deductible mileage using current IRS rates. Perfect for drivers, delivery workers, and mobile service providers.

TAX-SMART FROM DAY ONE
• Calculate your estimated tax liability in real-time
• Track business deductions throughout the year
• Export all your data to Excel for your accountant
• See your actual take-home pay after taxes and expenses

CALENDAR-BASED GIG MANAGEMENT
• Visual calendar shows all your scheduled gigs
• Color-coded status indicators (upcoming, completed, paid)
• Multi-day gig support for extended events
• Quick "Got Paid" workflow for marking gigs complete

COMPREHENSIVE REPORTING
• Monthly and yearly income summaries
• Expense breakdowns by category
• Mileage deduction calculations
• Business vs. reimbursed expense tracking

BUILT FOR GIG WORKERS
Whether you're a rideshare driver, event staff, freelance photographer, delivery driver, or independent contractor - Bookd helps you stay organized and tax-ready all year long.

NO SURPRISES AT TAX TIME
Stop scrambling for receipts and mileage logs when April arrives. With Bookd, everything you need for tax preparation is organized and ready to export.

SIMPLE & SECURE

Start tracking smarter today with Bookd - because knowing your numbers is the first step to growing your business.
